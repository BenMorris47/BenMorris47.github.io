Get To Know Me

I'm Ben Morris, a Programmer & Game Dev

Hi i'm Ben! a 3rd-year university student attending Falmouth University currently working towards a degree in Computing for games.

Over the past few years I've worked on improving my skills with a variety of programs and languages. I have become comfortable navigating and using software such as game engines (Unity/Unreal), 3D modeling programs (Blender/Maya), Version control (Github/Bitbucket), the Adobe Suites and many more. Utalising these skills I have created many small experimental projects and a couple of larger team based games. During this time I've discovered a keen interest in AI and procedural generation as well as electronics and robotics.

In my free time, I enjoy playing games such as Titanfall, Halo, Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and No Man Sky to name a few. As I continue my journey as a game developer, I am looking to find new opportunities to advance my knowledge and challenge my creative mindset.
