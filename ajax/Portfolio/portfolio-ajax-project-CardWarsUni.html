Card Wars

Project Info:

I was tasked with designing and creating a custom novel controller with an accompanying game. The device I made allowed for two 'portals' plugged into a computer that could receive input from RFID cards. These portals would give players goes to scan in cards from their game, loading them in and having them battle it out. Due to the prototype nature of this project, I was forced to sacrifice two lunch boxes.
